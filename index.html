Background

My family and I are originally from the Dominican Republic. A place where the weather is always warm and the people are always cheerful. We came to the US when I was a child, leaving behind everything for a brighter future. We settled in New York, where I was raised. I later moved to New Jersey.

I believe everyone has a favorite memory from their childhood. For me, it was the Christmas of 1997 when my parents surprised me with my first ever computer. It was a Compaq Presario. This event changed my life forever. Their gift gave life to my passion for technology and the Internet.
